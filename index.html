Col. Bobby Wallace History

Col. Bobby Wallace (Ret.) is a father to three daughters and a grandfather to eight grandchildren. He is a decorated veteran of the US Army Air Corps. He flew over 100 hours in combat while serving in Vietnam. He is a recipient of the Distinguished Flying Cross, the Bronze Star, and the Purple Heart. Bobby resides in Port Charlotte, Florida.
